My name is John Griffin and I am 35 years old. For most of my life, I used to be extremely overweight. I had an unhealthy diet. At one point, I weighed almost 300 pounds. One day, I looked at myself in the mirror and decided I need to change. I went on a weight loss journey. I started eating healthy and working out at the gym. I worked hard and never gave up. After a year, I lost more than 100 pounds. Today, I weigh 185 pounds. My goal is to inspire people.
